One Day

One Day showcases a day in the life of HCC Coleman College students. The video follows three students as they make their way to campus and then go about their day learning the skills they need to become the best lab techs, pharmacy technicians and radiographers they can be.

I developed the concept for this video. I wrote a treatment and created storyboards before shooting a second of footage. I casted the video. I shot the footage, even going so far as to rig up a simple Steadicam-like device out of some plumbing pipes. I edited the footage.

One Day was awarded a 2009 Silver Medallion Award in the promotional video category from the National Council for Marketing and Public Relations (NCMPR).
