This is HCC Coleman

Potential students have questions. It’s only natural. But it’s one thing to be told answers by a faculty or staff member or to read answers from a FAQ sheet. It’s another one to be given answers from fellow students who’ve been there and done that. That was the driving idea behind This is HCC Coleman.

I developed the concept for this video. I wrote the list of questions and created storyboards before shooting a second of footage. I casted the video. I shot and edited the footage.

This is HCC Coleman was awarded a 2010 Gold Medallion Award in the promotional video category from the National Council for Marketing and Public Relations (NCMPR).
